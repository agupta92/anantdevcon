About Us

Anant Devcon Pvt. Ltd. is a dedicated team of individuals coming from vivid backgrounds of construction, manufacturing and real estate, striving towards improving the quality of living. We strongly believe in yielding high-quality results in a timely, cost-effective and Eco-friendly manner.

For the past 15 years, we have been in the arena of construction by the names Anant Construction PVT. Ltd and ANJB Builders and Developers in various parts of Madhya Pradesh. After much experience, our founders joined the fields of manufacturing and the sales & installation of such units as well. Our own unit operates in Mandideep Industrial Area M.P. and various other parts of Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.
